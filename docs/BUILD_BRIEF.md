# Nordic Bistro: Build Brief

## 1. Project Overview
**Concept:** An immersive, WebGL-enhanced web experience for a luxury "New Nordic" dining concept.
**Goal:** To filter for high-value clientele by demanding their attention through a pristine, deeply atmospheric digital introduction before they can request a table.

## 2. Core Directives
- **Target Emotion:** Elevated Serenity
- **Primary CTA:** "Request Table"
- **Pacing:** Unhurried, deliberate. Elements should reveal themselves softly. 

## 3. The Content Pillars
Our narrative flows through three strict constraints, driving the user from atmospheric intrigue down to the conversion point.

### Pillar I: The Foraged Menu
- **Focus:** Rarity, terroir, and the extreme lengths taken to source ingredients in a harsh climate. 
- **Visuals:** Minimalist plating, stark contrasts of dark stone and raw seafood.

### Pillar II: The Chef's Philosophy
- **Focus:** Restraint. The idea that true luxury is knowing what to leave off the plate. 
- **Visuals:** High-contrast, moody portraits; sparks mapping to the "Fjord Fire" aesthetic.

### Pillar III: The Ambiance of the North
- **Focus:** The dining room as a sanctuary from the elements. 
- **Visuals:** Deep, glowing embers, monolithic textures, and a profound sense of quiet.

## 4. The Conversion Path (CTA)
Waitlists and standard reservations are inadequate. The primary action represents an inquiry, not a guarantee. 
- **Behavior:** The "Request Table" CTA must persist as a quiet but omnipresent escape hatch in the top navigation.
- **Tone:** High-touch concierge service.
